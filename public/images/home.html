Hi, my name is John Aitken. I am a Scottish freemason. I have collected Masonic tokens for many years now and my collection now contains over 600 Scottish tokens plus others from all over the world.  I have posted a list of the Scottish ones on this page. I am a member of the Mark Token Collectors Club (M.T.C.C.). I don't buy or sell tokens, I only swap them.  I have included my e-mail address below for any enquiries about tokens you may have. I have included images of some of my tokens for you to look at on the site, the site is frequently updated and I hope to post more images as soon as possible. The blue highlights are the links to the available photographs.
